Hi, I'm Brian

Mason & Muse: two names, just one me.

I'm a solo app developer in Vermont, and I launched Mason & Muse as the vehicle to release software I design and build here, in the woods, myself. My wife thought this was a terrible idea. Help me not embarrass myself!

We've known each other for a while now so I'll be honest. All those extra hours I spend getting something to align just right, or adding a little detail that isn't technically needed? It's not just for you. It's also for me. I wouldn't do this after all these years if I didn't love it. I do. It's a labor of love. There, I said it.
