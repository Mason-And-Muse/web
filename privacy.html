Privacy policy

Last updated: June 2026

This policy covers the Mason & Muse website (masonandmuse.com), operated by Mason & Muse, LLC. Each of my apps has its own privacy policy that governs how that app handles your information - see the app's App Store listing for details.

The short version

This website doesn't run analytics or tracking, sets no advertising cookies, and doesn't try to identify you or follow you around the web. Simple!

Server logs

The site is hosted on GitHub Pages. Like most web hosts, GitHub records standard technical request information (such as IP addresses and browser details) in its server logs. That handling is governed by GitHub's privacy statement, and I don't have access to individually identifiable analytics about your visit.

Email

If you contact me by email, I receive the information you choose to share (your email address and message) so I can respond. I use it only to reply to you and don't sell it or use it for marketing.

Changes

I may update this policy from time to time. When I do, I'll revise the date at the top of this page.
